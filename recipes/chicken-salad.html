Chicken Salad

Chicken salad is a simple and nutritious dish with the right portions of vegetables and protein. The portions here are for a three-people dish.

Ingredients

  • 500g of chicken breast.
  • 2tbsp
  • Six leaves of your favourite lettuce
  • One red tomato
  • One of the following: an apple, berries, a pear, or 100g walnuts
  • Dressing: choose as many as you want.
    • Honey mustard
    • Ketchup & mayo
    • Parmesan cheese
    • Ranch dressing
    • Vinaigrette

Preparation

  1. On a pan, add one tablespoon of butter and heat is slowly. Let it melt until the whole pan area is covered in butter.
  2. Place the chicken breasts on the pan until they are well cooked.
  3. While you wait, you can slice the lettuce, the tomatoes, and the fruits.
  4. When the breasts are ready, cut them in strips.
  5. On a bowl, place a bed of lettuce, followed by the tomato slices.
  6. Next add the chicken breasts.
  7. Finally, add the fruit and the dressing.
